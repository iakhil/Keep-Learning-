Keep Learning is an application that aims to help students learn their course material by extracting questions from a passage of text. This may ensure that the student is undertaking active learning and not just passively reading the text. The application allows:

  1. The user to provide a passage of text and produce a set of questions based on the passage.

  2. The user to provide a passage and ask questions whose answers are present in the passage.
